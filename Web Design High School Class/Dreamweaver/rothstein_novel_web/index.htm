Character
Information
 Bill "the Butcher" Poole A gang leader that was shot in the heart and abdomen and lived 14 days after that.
William "Boss" Tweed The boss of the gangs and he was a political leader. 
John Morrissey He was a very rich man, had $700,000. He was a rival of Bill Poole and he shot him. He got into a confrontation withe Bill in a bar.
 Anthony Rutger Obtained a 75 acre marsh, and it was eventually turned into the lower part of Manhattan. 
Edward Coleman He married a hot corn girl and shortly after murdered her.
Dan Kerrigan A noted Pugilist, and he operated a house at No. 110 Cherry Street.
John Fitch A sailor and captain of a ship that carried Robert Fulton, Chancellor Robert R. Livingston, John Hutchings, and Anthony Rutger.
John Hutchings 16-year-old boy who was on the ship to America, captained by John Fitch. 
Horace Greeley  A political leader. A wealthy investor. And a part of the uptown (Wealthy) gang.
