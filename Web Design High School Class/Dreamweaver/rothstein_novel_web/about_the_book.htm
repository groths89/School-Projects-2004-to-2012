Summary and Theme:

The Gangs of New York is about all of the different gangs in the late 1700s to the early 1900s. It is about the gangs of the Five Points, the Bowery, Manhattan, and Hell's Kitchen. The theme of the book is to not trust political leaders, because they are corrupted at times. they are against the police and with the gangs.

 

My Critique:

The book was a good book, it had action and adventure. It also had information on New York City and what it was like to be in the city back in history, the 1800s. However it was kind of a long book and somewhat boring.
